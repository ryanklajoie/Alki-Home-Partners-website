What We Offer

Core Service

Annual Home Partner Program

$1,000 / year — annual commitment
or
$1,500 one-time single engagement

Two scheduled visits per year, designed to build continuity across the life of your home. The annual price consolidates what most West Seattle homeowners already spend on fragmented exterior maintenance — gutter cleaning, pressure washing, ad hoc tasks. This is not a new cost. It is a better version of money you're already spending.

Visit 1 — Full Day

  • ✓
    Full Exterior Inspection

    Roof condition with photo documentation. Siding, drainage, and foundation review. Risk flagging for water intrusion, rot, and drainage failure.

  • ✓
    Exterior & Roof Pressure Wash

    Moss and algae removal. Non-structural debris clearing of roof and gutters.

  • ✓
    Written Condition Report

    Prioritized maintenance list with a "Fix Now / Monitor / Ignore" framework applied to every identified item. Delivered as a standalone professional document — not a sales pitch.

Visit 2 — Six Months Later (~1 hour)

  • ✓
    Property Walk Check-In

    Written update report noting any changes, emerging issues, or recommended action since Visit 1. This is how institutional memory of your home begins to accumulate.

One-Time Engagement Note

The $1,500 single engagement includes Visit 1 scope only (full inspection + wash + report). One-time clients who convert to the Annual Home Partner program receive a $500 credit toward project work.

Project Work

Remediation, Repair & Renovation

$100 / hour labor · materials at cost + 15%

Long-Term

Lifecycle Planning & Aging in Place

Ongoing develops across annual program years

After the first annual cycle, the relationship deepens. We build a multi-year capital roadmap aligned to your home's degradation timeline and your life stage. For clients approaching or in later life stages, we integrate aging-in-place planning — not as a separate service, but as a natural evolution of the relationship.

What Develops Over Time

  • ✓
    Annual Re-Inspection & Condition History

    A cumulative record of your home's condition across years — the kind of documentation that turns a pre-listing inspection into a sales asset rather than a liability.

  • ✓
    5-Year Capital Roadmap

    Phased planning that sequences your home's maintenance needs — "the roof has 3 years left; let's plan the exterior work for Year 4" — so you're never caught off guard.

  • ✓
    Aging in Place Integration

    Invisible universal design. Safety without clinical appearance. Technology integration. A home that adapts to your needs so you can stay in it.

  • ✓
    Preferred Contractor Access

    Priority scheduling with vetted tradespeople from our established network. No more referral roulette.
